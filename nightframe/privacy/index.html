Nightframe Privacy Policy

Nightframe is designed as a local-first app. This policy explains how the app handles session data, exports, backups, and device settings.

Support Home
What Nightframe stores
Session data
Session notes, targets, locations, capture settings, issues, and related records are stored locally on your device so the app can work offline.
Rig and recipe data
Reusable rigs, optical train notes, recipes, and calibration records remain on the device unless you export them.
User exports
Exported PDFs, CSVs, JSON files, and backup files are created at your request and remain under your control.
App settings
Preferences are stored locally so the app can keep your chosen workflow in place.
What Nightframe does not do
No account required
You do not need to create an account, sign in, or connect a cloud profile.
No analytics or ad tracking
Nightframe does not use advertising identifiers, analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking.
No server-side user profile
The app does not maintain a server-side record of your sessions or equipment.
No subscription or IAP
Nightframe is a one-time purchase app and does not implement in-app purchases.
Files and backups

When you export a backup, report, or CSV file, iOS places that file wherever you choose. Nightframe does not upload it to a Nightframe-owned server.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The latest version will always stay at this URL.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@nightframe.app. Include your device model and a short description of the issue so we can respond quickly.
