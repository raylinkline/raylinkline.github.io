What Nightframe does

Nightframe helps serious deep-sky imagers log sessions, track rigs, record capture settings, capture calibration details, and turn hard-won lessons into reusable recipes.

Local by design
Sessions, rigs, recipes, issues, capture settings, and calibration records stay on device unless you export them.
Built for review and reuse
The app keeps successful nights organized so you can reuse what worked next time.
Language aware
Nightframe follows the system language and supports English, Simplified Chinese, Japanese, Korean, German, Italian, Russian, and French.
For App Review

Nightframe is a one-time purchase app. There is no free tier, no subscription, and no StoreKit purchase flow.

No Restore Purchases flow
The app does not use in-app purchases, so there is no Restore Purchases button.
No account or cloud login
Nightframe does not require an account, sign-in, or server-side profile.
Offline workflows
Export, import, backup, and restore all work entirely inside the app.
Review data
Test data can be created manually through the UI. No server setup is needed.
Need help?

Send the device model, iOS version, a short description, and a screenshot to support@nightframe.app.

Privacy at a glance

Nightframe keeps user data on device by default, does not track across apps, and does not maintain a server-side user profile.
